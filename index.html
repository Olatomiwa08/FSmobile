About Fuel Station
Online (FSO)

Fuel Station Online is a collaborative platform for managing fuel stations. It facilitates electronic record keeping for all daily transactions (meter

reading, product delivery, dipstick measurement and cash lodgment). You can generate business reports, review business performance by stations and view products stocks level anywhere and anytime.

Why Use Fuel Station Online?

Security

Hosted in a secured cloud environment with facilities to automatically back up all your valuable data.

Automated

Tanks calibration parameters are stored in the system which automatically converts readings (product height) from the dipstick into volume in liters, thus eliminating the need for the use of manual calibration chat.

Performance Analysis

You can set up monthly revenue targets for filling stations and track their performance online from anywhere.

Fraud Prevention

Our solution comes with internal reconciliation mechanisms that ensures that all meter readings are reconciled with tank dipping and that all payments collected are accounted for at the end of the day.  There is no room for  fraudulent dipstick measurements!

Standardized Reports

You can order end-of-day summary or detailed reports as well as transaction reports. Fuel Station Online generates reports using standardized templates for all your stations.

Collaboration

 Attendants can record initial and closing meter reading, payment collected from POS, total cash collected and so on. The Manager will review these transactions and approve as appropriate. At the end of the day, the Manager will run the END-OF-DAY routine which automatically sends daily summary reports to the dealer.

STEP 1: USER REGISTRATION

As a dealer or company representative, you can setup an account to manage all your filing stations by providing basic information such as your name and a valid email address. This email address is your user Id to log in to filling station online.

You also need to provide a password to your account. We recommend that you enter a very strong password and keep you userId and password confidential at all time.

STEP 2: COMPANY INFORMATION

The first time you log in, you will be required to provide the following:

  • Add Company Information.
  • Setup Products
  • Add Users (Station Manger)
  • Add Stations
Company information:
You may provide the following information
  • Company name: Provide the company name
  • Address: This the company head office address
  • Phone number: please enter a phone that can receive sms for daily business summary. Enter the number in international format e.g. For 09033717381 enter the number as +2349033717381
  • Fax number: Provide the fax number
  • Commission: This is the amount (in your currency) of commission you receive on 1 litter of fuel. The default value is 1.3 which is the amount of commission for selling 1 litter of fuel in Nigeria (Naira)
  • Opening time: This will be the opening time for all your stations.
  • Closing time" for all stations
  • Currency: Select your local currency
  • Decimal place: Select the level of precision for calculations.

Manage Products:
You are required to setup products that will be available to all your station. You can do this by selecting products from our predefined list and give them your own names. For example you can select PMS from our list and name it Petro or Gas depending on the local nomenclature for the product. Also important is the product price per litre in your local currency.

Application Users:
If you have more than one filling stations or you want to give access to other users, you can create the users by entering their basic information. You need to enter the user names (First name and last name), valid email address and valid phone number. Filling station online platform will generate an id and a temporary password for the user and email the details to the user. The user will be able to change their password and log on to the system. However, creating a user does not automatically grant the user an access to any of your filling station, you will need to explicitly specify the user as the Manager of any station you want him or her to access. One user can be the manager of 1 or more stations.

Creating a Station
You can station to you account by providing the name of the station, State or region where the station is located and (optionally) the supply a Manager to the station. You or other users will then be able to configure the station later.

Configuring your Stations
After the initial setup, you will be directed to a page where you can provide more information to each of your station. Station configuration includes the following
  • Adding Station Address
  • Adding underground tanks
  • Adding Pumps and connecting pumps to Tanks
You can also open the page by clicking on the Edit button beside the station name on your dashboard.

Adding Tanks
Please enter a descriptive name for your tank. We recommend that you use the same name convention that you currently use at the station. Every tank you create must be linked to a product that the tank is used for. You also need to specify the maximum capacity of the tank.

Adding Pumps
Enter a descriptive name for the pump and select the tank that the pump is connected to.

STEP 3: TRANSACTIONS

After the initial setup and configuration, recording daily transaction as simple as :
  1. Record your daily meter reading
  2. Record product delivery (if any)
  3. Record dipping stick measurements
  4. Record your cash lodgement.
